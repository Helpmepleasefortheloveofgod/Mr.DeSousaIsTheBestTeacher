Antoine Lavoisier

  • Home



    • 1.) When did they live? Where did they live?
     Born August 26, 1743, Paris, France

    2.) What new information did they contribute to the understanding of the atom?
     Developed an experimentally based theory of the chemical reactivity of oxygen and co authored the modern system for naming chemical substances.

    3.) How did they find this new information? (What experiments did they do?)
     In experiments with phosphorus and sulfur, both of which burned readily, Lavoisier showed that they gained weight by combining with air. And with lead calx, he was able to capture a large amount of air that was liberated when the calx was heated.
